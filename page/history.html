Welcome to Siem Reap

Discover the cultural heart of Cambodia, home to the world-renowned Angkor Wat temples, vibrant markets, and a rich historical heritage.

History of Siem Reap

Siem Reap has a rich history dating back to the Khmer Empire, with its legacy preserved in the magnificent temples of Angkor Wat. The city's name, meaning "Defeat of Siam," reflects its storied past.

Today, Siem Reap blends ancient traditions with modern tourism, offering visitors a glimpse into its fascinating heritage.

Top Attractions

Angkor Wat

The crown jewel of Cambodia, this temple complex is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit landmark.

Pub Street

Experience the vibrant nightlife, delicious local cuisine, and lively entertainment in the heart of Siem Reap.

Floating Villages

Explore the unique lifestyle of communities living on the Tonle Sap Lake, a stunning natural wonder.

Cultural Tips

  • Always dress modestly when visiting temples.
  • Learn a few basic Khmer phrases to connect with locals.
  • Respect local customs, including removing shoes before entering homes or temples.

Local Dining Experiences

Amok Curry

A traditional Khmer dish made with coconut milk, spices, and fish, often served in a banana leaf bowl.

Nom Banh Chok

A popular breakfast dish of rice noodles topped with a fragrant fish-based green curry sauce.

Street Food Tours

Join a guided tour to sample a variety of street foods, from fried tarantulas to sweet sticky rice desserts.

Adventure Activities

Hot Air Balloon Rides

Soar above the Angkor Wat complex and enjoy breathtaking views of the temples and surrounding jungle.

Quad Biking

Explore the countryside and visit remote villages on an exhilarating quad bike adventure.
